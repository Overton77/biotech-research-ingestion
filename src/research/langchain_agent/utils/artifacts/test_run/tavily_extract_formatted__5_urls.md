# Environmental Monitoring

Environmental Monitoring (EM) is crucial to mitigating risks in your facility. Analytical Resource Laboratories has the expertise to assist you with developing and implementing your EM program and validating your cleaning processes.

[Get a customized quote](/rfq/)

The first step is to determine your risks and assess your risk tolerances. This process might include the following questions:

1. Which products do we produce which pose the greatest threat to cross-contamination?
2. Do our products contain allergens, and how do we clean thoroughly enough to remove allergens from other parts of the facility and other products?
3. Which equipment should we use to test the cleanliness of our machines following a full cleaning? How often should we monitor our environmental conditions?
4. Based on these and other questions, ARL can formulate a detailed plan that will help you meet your quality objectives.

### Most Commonly Requested Environmental Monitoring Analyses

1. Critical Control Points
2. Microbial Swabbing

# Microbiology Testing

Analytical Resource Labs (ARL) offers comprehensive microbiological testing services to safeguard the quality and safety of your products. Our state-of-the-art technology, including PCR, delivers fast and accurate results you can trust. ARL is ISO/IEC 17025:2017 accredited, ensuring adherence to the highest industry standards.

[Request a custom quote](https://www.analyticalresource.com/rfq/)

Microbiological contamination can pose a serious threat to your products and brand reputation. ARL's meticulous microbiological testing services help identify and mitigate these risks, ensuring your products meet the safety standards.

## Advanced Technology for Accurate Results

ARL leverages cutting-edge technology, including Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR), to provide unmatched accuracy and speed in microbiological testing. Unlike traditional methods, PCR can detect even the smallest traces of contaminants, allowing for early intervention and corrective actions.

## Extensive Experience and Expertise

Our team of highly skilled microbiologists possesses in-depth knowledge and experience in various testing methodologies. We stay updated on the latest advancements in the field to ensure our services remain at the forefront of the industry.

## Unwavering Commitment to Quality

ARL is committed to providing the highest quality microbiological testing services. We maintain accreditations from leading organizations like the AOAC (Association of Official Analytical Chemists) and USP (United States Pharmacopeia), demonstrating our dedication to meeting the strictest quality standards

## Streamlined Testing Process

We understand the importance of efficiency. ARL offers a streamlined testing process to deliver timely results without compromising on accuracy. Our clear communication keeps you informed throughout the process.

## Tailored Solutions to Meet Your Needs

ARL recognizes that every client has unique requirements. We provide a comprehensive suite of microbiological testing services and customize them to address your specific needs and concerns.

*   USP 51
*   E coli
*   Staphylococcus aureus

*   Rapid 3-day Yeast and Mold
*   USP and PCPC Preservative Efficacy Testing
