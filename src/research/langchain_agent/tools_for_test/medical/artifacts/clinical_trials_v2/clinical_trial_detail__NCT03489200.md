=== ClinicalTrials.gov Study Detail ===

NCT03489200 | EH301 for the Treatment of ALS
Status: COMPLETED
Lead sponsor: Elysium Health
Conditions: Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis
Interventions: EH301, Placebo
Collaborators: Fundación Universidad Católica de Valencia San Vicente Mártir, University of Valencia
Study URL: https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT03489200

Brief summary:
The objective of this trial is to evaluate the efficacy and tolerability of EH301 in patients with amyotrophic lateral sclerosis. Patients with ALS are randomized to receive either EH301 or placebo daily and undergo active evaluation for 6 months.
